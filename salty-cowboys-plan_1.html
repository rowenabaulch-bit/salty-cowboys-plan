Salty Cowboys

A calmer way to handle bookings.

Right now requests reach you across WhatsApp, email and Instagram — often missing a date, a weight, or an experience level, so you're piecing the booking together before you can even answer. This is a plan to fix that, built in small steps so it never gets in the way of how you already run things.

i.

You stay in control. Nothing is ever booked without your yes — every request waits for you.

ii.

Built in phases. Each step has to genuinely make your week easier before we build the next one.

iii.

A helper, not a takeover. It tidies up what's coming in. You still run every booking your own way.

The plan

Four stops along the same trail. We're standing at the first — the rest only happen if the one before it has earned its place.

Phase 1 Starting here

Every request arrives complete.

What changesEach booking reaches you as one clean WhatsApp message with everything in it — activity, date, the rider's name, age, weight, experience, and any notes.
What it savesNo more decoding scattered messages or chasing people for the one detail they left out before you can decide.

Nothing about how you reply or run bookings changes — you just stop getting half-finished requests.

Phase 2

Customers can see available dates (testing calendar integration)

What changesPeople can see which days and times you're not available, so they stop asking for slots you can't do — a "pending" booking slot can still be booked over if someone pays first.
Your privacyThey only ever see "unavailable" — never what you're doing, or who else has booked.
Phase 3

Requests land gently in your calendar.

What changesA request can drop straight into your calendar marked pending — held lightly, never confirmed, and never permanently blocking a slot.
Your controlYou still accept, decline or ask questions over WhatsApp, exactly as you do now. Nothing is confirmed until you say so.

Behind the scenes it quietly matches the right horse and stablehand to each rider's experience — the customer never picks for themselves.

Phase 4

Payments and donations.

What changesCustomers can pay for a booking, and supporters can donate to the rescue.
On your termsPayment is only ever taken after you've approved a booking — so you're never refunding someone you've turned down.

How we'll know it's working

We only move to the next stop if the one before it is genuinely helping you. If Phase 1 doesn't make your week lighter, we pause and rethink.

What I'd love to ask you

Just so we can tell whether this is really saving you time:

  • i.Roughly how many booking requests do you get in a week or month?
  • ii.Of those, how many turn up missing something you have to chase?
  • iii.You don't have to tell me — but if you can record roughly how much profit these inquiries bring in each month, we can measure any impact 3 months to a year from now.

This starts small on purpose. Phase 1 alone should mean calmer, complete bookings — and we go further only if it earns it.
